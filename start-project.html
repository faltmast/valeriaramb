Ready to start? Here's what we need.

We take the time to get things right. To start efficiently, have the following ready.

Project Checklist

What to prepare before we talk.

Architectural Drawings

Complete architectural plans with dimensions and specifications. Floor plans, elevations, and sections — even preliminary versions help us understand scale and layout.

Site Survey & Soil Report

Topographic survey and geotechnical investigation report. SPT boring logs or soil analysis — this determines foundation type and depth.

Project Timeline

Expected start date, milestones, and delivery deadline. We plan backwards from your deadline to ensure on-time delivery.

Budget Range

Approximate budget helps us propose the most efficient structural solution calibrated to your financial reality.

Permits & Approvals

Existing permits, municipal approvals, or regulatory requirements. Any environmental or zoning constraints we should know about.

Hydromechanical Plans

If available, hydromechanical or electrical-mechanical documentation. These help us coordinate structural openings and load requirements.
