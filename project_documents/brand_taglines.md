# NU GUA — Brand Tagline (All Languages)

Core concept: Zoom out / Zoom in — seeing the big picture AND the details.

---

## English
"We zoom out to see what others miss. We zoom in so nothing goes wrong."

## Portuguese
"Olhamos de longe para enxergar o que outros ignoram. Olhamos de perto para que nada escape ao controle."

## Spanish
"Alejamos la mirada para ver lo que otros no ven. Nos acercamos para que nada salga mal."

## Mandarin
放眼全局，捕捉盲点。深耕细节，排除风险。
(Fàngyǎn quánjú, bǔzhuō mángdiǎn. Shēngēng xìjié, páichú fēngxiǎn.)
"Broaden the view to catch blind spots. Deeply cultivate details to eliminate risks."
