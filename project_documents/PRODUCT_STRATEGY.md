## Vision & Purpose

### What Are We Building?
A bilingual (PT/EN) website for Valeria Ramb's structural engineering company, repositioning from solo entrepreneur to professional organization.

### Why Does This Exist?
1. **Credibility check** — construction companies Google her after seeing her bid. The website must prove she can deliver.
2. **Recruitment signal** — she's scaling from entrepreneur to organization, needs to attract talent.
3. **Client education** — explain what structural engineering does and what clients need to bring to start a project.

### Who Is This For?
**Primary:** Construction companies evaluating her bid for infrastructure projects in Brazil.
**Secondary:** International companies wanting to build in Brazil. Architects and engineers needing a structural partner.

## Positioning

### The Positioning Statement
We help architects, engineers, and construction companies bring their infrastructure and construction projects to reality by seeing the full picture — zooming out to catch what others miss, zooming in so nothing goes wrong.

### #1 Weapon: Big Picture Thinking
The zoom in/zoom out metaphor is the brand. She sees the full picture where competitors get lost in details or miss critical points.

### What Makes Her Different
- Other firms: get lost, miss the critical point, don't see the big picture, don't fully understand the data
- Valeria Ramb: zooms out to see full picture, zooms in on details, meets deadlines, balances what's needed with what's available

### What Clients Say
- "We like that you get involved in the project" — asks questions, participates in decisions, takes ownership and responsibility

### Company Name: NU GUA (Serviço Estrutural)
From Chinese mythology — Nu Gua repaired the pillars of heaven and the broken corners of earth after the rebel Gong Gong destroyed them. She used the feet of a tortoise and melted-down stones. She also built a palace that became a prototype for walled cities. A mythological structural engineer. The name connects directly to the company's purpose: repairing, building, and holding things together.

### Tone of Voice
**Bold, warm, technical**

### Brand Personality
Human but strict. "Like being a father and mother at the same time." Visitors should feel heard. The company takes time until things are right.

## Target Audience & Positioning

### Who Is This For?
Construction companies bidding on infrastructure projects in Brazil. Decision-makers who need a structural engineer they can trust to deliver on time.

### What Are They Worried About?
- Can she deliver at this scale?
- Will she meet the deadline?
- Does she have the background and track record?

### What They Check Before Choosing
- Background and recommendations

Infrastructure projects use a bid/auction system. Companies submit bids, and the client chooses. The website is the credibility check after they see the bid.

### Competitive Positioning
**We are NOT:** The cheapest option. Grey and bare minimum like everyone else.

**We ARE:** The firm that sees what others miss. Detail-oriented, deadline-reliable, and fully invested in your project.

## Website Architecture

### Pages
1. **Home** — Hero positioning, services overview, featured projects, CTA
2. **About** — Valeria + Sergio together, company story, values
3. **Why Structural Engineering?** — Educate clients on what a structural engineer does and why it matters
4. **Our Approach** — Values, zoom in/zoom out method, ownership, involvement, deadlines
5. **Projects** — 5 showcase projects with photos and details
6. **Start Your Project** — Checklist of what clients need to provide before starting
7. **Contact** — Form + location (São Paulo)

### Design Direction
- **Color:** Dark blue base
- **Style:** Minimal, futuristic, modern — competitors are "all grey with bare minimum"
- **Visuals:** Illustrations and diagrams (not photography-heavy)
- **Feel:** Human but strict, professional but caring
- **Logo:** Needs to be created
- **Language:** Bilingual PT/EN with toggle
