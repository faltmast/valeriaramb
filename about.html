Who We Are

An organization built for precision.

NU GUA is a structural engineering firm headquartered in São Paulo, delivering infrastructure and construction projects at national scale. We combine deep technical expertise with big-picture thinking to ensure every structure we touch is safe, efficient, and built to last.

The Origin

The Nu Gua Story

In Chinese mythology, Nu Gua is the goddess who repaired the pillars of heaven when they cracked and the corners of the earth when they broke. She melted down five-colored stones to patch the sky, and she cut the legs of a giant tortoise to use as new pillars holding the heavens in place.

She didn’t just repair — she built. Legend says she constructed a palace that became the prototype for the first walled cities, establishing the foundations of civilization itself.

Like Nu Gua, we repair, we build, and we hold things together. We take what is broken and make it whole. We take what exists only on paper and make it stand. That is the work of structural engineering — invisible when done right, essential always.

“She repaired the pillars of heaven and the broken corners of earth.”

The Team

The people behind the structure.

Valeria Ramb

Founder & Lead Structural Engineer

Valéria Ramb is a structural engineer and BIM specialist with over a decade of experience in the field. Her work is defined by a balance of technical rigor and practical site experience, ensuring that complex structural analyses translate into efficient, high-performance results. She has spent much of her career designing reinforced concrete and steel structures for major clients, while also leading technical inspections throughout Brazil. Now working as a consultant, she focuses on creating detailed, buildable designs that prioritize material efficiency and smoother construction workflows. Alongside her engineering firm, she teaches Engineering and Mathematics in an international setting, driven by a commitment to fostering critical thinking and curiosity in her students.

Sergio

Co-founder & Senior Infrastructure Engineer

Sérgio Brandão brings over two decades of experience in structural engineering. Having worked alongside Valéria for 11 years at Oficina de Engenharia, he brings the depth of an experienced track record to NU GUA. His expertise spans reinforced concrete and steel structures for major clients including Banco Itaú, CREA, and FDE.
